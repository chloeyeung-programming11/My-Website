CEOs

Bernard Arnault, CEO of LVMH, has led the company since 1987, expanding its luxury portfolio across various sectors and reinforcing its global leadership.

In contrast, François-Henri Pinault, CEO of Kering since 2005, has focused on transforming the brand into a leader in sustainable luxury, emphasizing ethical practices and innovation.

Marco Bizzarri is an Italian businessman known for his role as the CEO of Gucci. Under his leadership, the brand has seen significant growth and revitalization, particularly with a focus on innovative marketing and inclusivity. He has played a key role in shaping Gucci's contemporary identity, collaborating with creative director Alessandro Michele.

Pietro Beccari is an Italian businessman known for his role as the CEO of Dior. Before taking on this position in 2022, he served as the CEO of Fendi and held various leadership roles within the LVMH group. Beccari is recognized for his expertise in luxury brand management and has been instrumental in driving growth and innovation within the fashion industry.
